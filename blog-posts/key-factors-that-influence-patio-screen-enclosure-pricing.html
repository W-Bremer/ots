Key Factors That Influence Patio Screen Enclosure Pricing

August 14, 2025

Adding a patio enclosure, screen patio, or swimming pool screen enclosure is one of the smartest upgrades Florida homeowners can make. Offering year-round enjoyment, bug protection, and enhanced curb appeal. But how much does it cost?

While every enclosure is unique, several key elements influence the total price. At Optimal Thermal Structures, we design everything from basic screen patios to luxury lanai screen enclosures, and we’re here to help you understand the factors behind your investment.

Top Factors That Affect Patio Enclosure Costs

1.  Size of the Enclosure

The most straightforward cost factor is size. Larger screen enclosures, whether for your pool, lanai, or patio require more materials and labor. We work on everything from compact screen porch enclosures to expansive swimming pool enclosures that turn backyards into outdoor retreats.

A typical starting point is a 10x10 (100 sq ft) layout, but our screen patio contractors can handle custom builds of any scale.

2. Screen Type

Not all screens are created equal. Basic mesh is the most affordable, but many clients opt for:

Phifer Pet Screen (great for homes with animals)

Privacy screen (for extra seclusion)

Solar screen (to block heat and glare)

These options are perfect for lanai screen enclosures, screen patios, and swimming pool screen enclosures. The more durable or specialized the screen, the higher the initial cost but the long-term value often outweighs the price.

3. Roofing Requirements

Do you already have a roof? That saves you money. If not, you’ll need to choose between several roof types:

Screen roof

Insulated aluminum roof

Aluminum patio covers

We also offer design options including mansard, gable, and dome roofs, popular choices for Florida homes due to their structure and airflow advantages.

4. Flooring & Foundation

If you're building on an existing concrete slab, great, it cuts down on cost. If not, flooring will be part of the project. Options include:

Basic concrete

Decorative texture slabs

Stylish pavers

The foundation not only affects pricing but also impacts the appearance and functionality of your screen patio or screen enclosure.

5. Add-On Features

Want your patio enclosure to feel like a true extension of your home? Consider optional features such as:

Ceiling fans

Lighting

Electrical outlets

Motorized patio screens

Decorative trim

Aluminum Retractable Awnings or aluminum carports

These upgrades increase both comfort and cost, but they’re ideal for anyone creating a personalized outdoor living space.

Common Price Range Examples

Here’s how these elements influence your budget:

Entry-Level Option

Enclose an existing screen patio or lanai

Standard mesh screen

Minimal customization

Ideal for tight budgets

Mid-Range Build

Moderate-size screen enclosure from scratch

Upgraded screen material like pet screen or privacy screen

Includes roofing (mansard/gable/dome or aluminum patio covers)

Add-on features like lighting or fans

Premium Custom Build

Large screened patio enclosure or swimming pool screen enclosure

High-end materials like pavers or decorative concrete

Insulated roof panels or custom aluminum awnings

Includes full electrical, lighting, and luxury features

For homeowners creating a year-round outdoor oasis

What’s the ROI?

In Florida, adding a screen patio, pool enclosure, or lanai screen often delivers an ROI of 80% to 120%. It’s more than just added comfort, it increases usable square footage and boosts resale value. Plus, with features like aluminum awnings, privacy screens, and retractable patio screens, your home becomes more appealing to future buyers.
