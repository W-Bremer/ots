How Much Does a Patio Enclosure Cost in Florida?

August 14, 2025

Dreaming of enjoying the outdoors without battling bugs, heat, or sudden downpours? A screened patio enclosure or swimming pool screen enclosure can turn that dream into a year-round reality. But before the relaxing evenings and weekend hangouts can begin, most Florida homeowners have one big question:

“How much will a patio enclosure cost?”

The truth is, there’s no one-size-fits-all answer, and that’s actually a good thing. Your screen patio or lanai screen enclosure is a custom project. Your preferences, space, and materials will all shape the final price. At Optimal Thermal Structures, we believe in helping you understand your options clearly, so you can make the best decision for your budget and lifestyle.

Let’s break it down.

Average Cost of Patio Enclosures in Florida

In general, screen porch enclosures in Florida range from $8 to $30 per square foot. That number can swing based on multiple factors, including whether you're working with an existing concrete slab, what screen type you choose (like pet screen or privacy screen), and the features you want to include (think retractable patio screens, lighting, or custom doors).

Working with experienced screen patio contractors (like our team at Optimal Thermal Structures!) helps you find a smart balance between cost, quality, and function.

What Affects the Cost of a Patio Enclosure?

Here are the key cost drivers to keep in mind when planning your screen enclosure project:

1. Size of the Enclosure

No surprise, larger screen patios or swimming pool enclosures mean more materials and labor. Whether it's a cozy lanai screen enclosure or a full pool screen enclosure, size directly affects the budget.

2. Roofing Needs

Already have a roof? That’s a big cost-saver. But if your enclosure requires a new structure, you’ll need to decide between types like mansard, gable, dome, or flat roof designs. We specialize in aluminum patio covers and insulated roofs that can handle Florida’s harsh sun and sudden storms.

3. Screen Type

Not all screens are created equal. We offer everything from standard mesh to Phifer Pet Screen, privacy screen, and solar-blocking materials. Upgraded screen options are especially popular for swimming pool screen enclosures and screen patios with pets or high sun exposure.

4. Foundation & Flooring

Will we build on an existing concrete slab, or are you planning to upgrade with pavers, tile, or even decorative screen patio flooring? Flooring can significantly impact cost and visual appeal.

5. Add-Ons & Extras

Want to create a true outdoor living area? Add aluminum awnings, lighting, fans, outlets, or even custom enclosures. We also offer retractable patio screens, which give you the flexibility of open air or total coverage depending on your mood and the weather.

Example Budget Ranges

Budget-Friendly ($)

Basic screen porch enclosure using standard materials over an existing slab

Ideal for small patios or lanai screen projects

Mid-Range ($$)

Medium-sized screen enclosure with upgraded roofing like a mansard or gable roof, and optional paver flooring

Great for screen patio contractors and homeowners seeking durability and comfort

Premium ($$$)

Large swimming pool enclosure with custom screening, aluminum patio covers, decorative pavers, and integrated electrical work

Ideal for those building a full-scale outdoor living room

What’s the ROI of a Screen Enclosure?

You might be surprised to learn that screen patio enclosures can deliver an ROI of 80–120%. Especially in Florida, where outdoor living is a way of life, screen enclosures, aluminum awnings, and aluminum carports not only improve comfort, they also boost home value and curb appeal.
