Protect your pool screen enclosure or pool cage year-round with a durable, modern screen enclosure that blocks debris, insects, and harsh weather while enhancing outdoor comfort.

A pool enclosure (or pool cage) transforms your outdoor space into a protected, low-maintenance, and year-round retreat. Built with high-quality aluminum framing and weather-resistant screening, our custom enclosures keep your pool clean, safe, and comfortable. Frequently Asked Questions

What are the advantages of installing a pool enclosure?



A pool enclosure provides multiple benefits beyond just covering the pool. It keeps out insects, leaves, and debris, making pool maintenance easier and less time-consuming. It creates a safer environment by acting as a barrier that reduces the risk of accidental falls into the pool. Enclosures also offer UV protection, helping shield swimmers from excessive sun exposure. With the right design, they extend your swimming season by regulating temperature and reducing evaporation. Additionally, they enhance the aesthetics of your backyard while boosting overall property value.

What materials are commonly used in pool enclosures?



Pool enclosures are typically built with aluminum framing and fiberglass mesh or polyester screening. Aluminum is lightweight yet durable, making it ideal for large spans and open designs without warping or rusting. The screening material is UV-resistant and designed to withstand outdoor exposure while allowing airflow and visibility. Some screen options are designed for extra strength, solar shading, or fine mesh that blocks tiny insects like no-see-ums. Together, these materials create a long-lasting enclosure that requires minimal maintenance.

How much maintenance does a pool enclosure require?



Maintenance for a pool enclosure is generally minimal compared to an open pool. Screens should be rinsed periodically to remove dirt, dust, or pollen buildup. Frames may need occasional inspection to check for signs of wear, loose fasteners, or corrosion. If a screen tears, it can usually be repaired or rescreened without replacing the entire structure. Regular cleaning of the frame and panels helps extend the enclosure’s lifespan. With simple upkeep, a pool enclosure can last for many years while keeping your pool clean and enjoyable.

Can a pool enclosure be customized to fit any backyard?



Yes, pool enclosures can be fully customized to match the size, shape, and design of any backyard. Options include low-rise, high-rise, or panoramic styles depending on the level of openness you prefer. Homeowners can choose from different screen types, roof styles, and frame finishes to create a look that blends seamlessly with their home. Customization also allows for additional features such as doors, lighting, and even integration with existing patios or decks. This flexibility ensures that every pool enclosure is both functional and visually appealing.
